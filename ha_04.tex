Deadline: 2026-07-07, 14:59. 

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Solve the recurence equation:
    \[
    a_n + 6 a_{n-1} + 9 a_{n-2} = 0, \quad a_0 = 5, a_1 = 10.
    \]
    \item Solve the recurence equation:
    \[
    c_n + 6 c_{n-1} + 10 c_{n-2} = 0, \quad c_0 = 0, c_1 = 10.
    \]
    \item Solve the recurrence equation: 
    \[
    a_n = (n + 5) a_{n-1}, \quad a_1 = 100.
    \]
    You may certainly use factorial in your answer! 
    \item I enter a casino in Las Vegas with $50$ tokens. I play the roulette.
I always bet one token on black. I will leave the casino when my fortune will be $70$ tokens or $0$ tokens. 

In a typical Las Vegas roulette there are $38$ pockets: $18$ black pockets, $18$ red pockets, one zero and one double zero.
Let $p_n$ be the probability that I will leave the casino as winner if I have $n$ tokens now. 
\begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*)]
    \item What are the initial conditions $p_0$ and $p_{70}$?
    \item Build the recurrence equation for $p_n$. 
    \item Solve the recurrence equation for $p_n$.
    \item Evaluate $p_{50}$ up to $2$ digits. 
\end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}
