\subsection*{Home assignment: day 10 $\to$ day 13}

Deadline: 2026-07-15, 16:59. 
You can use sympy or any other software for symbolic computations.

\begin{enumerate}
\item Consider a bracelet with $9$ beads. Two bracelets are considered the same if they can be transformed one into another by rotations or flips.  
\begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*)]
\item How many rotations (including the trivial $0$-degrees rotation) are there?
\item How many flips (reflections) are there?
\item Calculate the cycle index for the group of actions. 
\item How many different bracelets can you make with $k$ gemstone types available?
\item How many different bracelets can you make with exactly $3$ diamonds, $3$ amethysts and $3$ charoits?
\end{enumerate}

\end{enumerate}
